About Lexis Tools

Privacy-first AI tools for everyone

Our Mission

At Lexis Tools, we believe that powerful AI should be accessible to everyone, without sacrificing privacy or control. Our platform empowers you to leverage the best AI technologies, such as OpenAI, Google, and Groq, using your own API keys. This ensures that your data remains private and that you maintain full control over your usage and costs.

Why We Built Lexis Tools

Traditional AI platforms often come with hidden costs, data privacy concerns, and restrictive limitations. We wanted to create a better way.

With Lexis Tools, you:

  • Use your own API keys, so your content is never stored on our servers.
  • Pay only the standard rates set by your AI provider, with no hidden fees
  • Have complete control over the models and tools you choose.
  • Benefit from privacy by design, with no tracking or data analysis.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to maintaining Lexis Tools as a free, privacy-focused platform. We promise to never:

  • Store or analyze your content.
  • Sell your data to third parties.
  • Charge subscription fees for essential features.
  • Track your usage beyond what is necessary.
  • Compromise on security or privacy.
